servings: 4
prep: 10M
cook: 10M
ingredients:
  - 100g all-purpose floured surface
  - 1 cup water
  - 1 egg
  - 1 tsp dashi powder (granules)
  - 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 100g fish cake
  - 1 bunch of green onions
  - 1/2 teaspoon takoyaki sauce
  - 1/2 cup katsuobuisi (bonito flakes)
  - Japanese mayo
---

1. Mix ingredients for batter in a bowl
1. Warm a takoyaki on medium-high
2. Oil each of the spheres
3. Pour batter into the pan (about 3/4 full). It's ok to spill over
4. Add fillings
5. Cook until setting and turn 1/4 turn (2 toothpicks work)
6. Pour in more batter and repeat
7. Keep turning until there is a nice ball formed

See <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yjyddfwijPM&feature=youtu.be&t=183> for technique
