ingredients:
  - Whole Chicken
  - 2 star anise
  - 5cm ginger (sliced)
  - 1 bulb of garlic, halved
  - 1/3 cup Chinese cooking wine
  - 1/4 cup white sugar
  - 1/3 cup soy sauce
---

Put chicken in pot and cover in water

Add aromatics

Place over medium heat and bring to a boil

Boil and cook for 15 minutes

Remove from heat and let stand for 1 hour.
