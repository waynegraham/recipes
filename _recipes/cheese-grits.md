## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cup of chicken broth
- 1/2 cup of milk
- salt
- pepper
- 1/2 cup grits
- 2 green onions
- smoked paprika
- Cheddar Cheese
- Parmesan
- Couple dashes of hot sauce

## Directions

- Bring milk and broth to a boil
- Add a pinch of salt
- Add grits
- Cover and reduce to medium-low heat and simmer
- Stir every so often until done (like 5 minutes)
- Add the rest
